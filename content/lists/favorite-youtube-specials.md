### Brad Wenzel -- Desperate Times
Brad is a Detroit comic. I opened for him when he did a run through Wisconsin at Janesville Performing Arts Center and the Gravity Box in Mauston. Very nice guy and fantastic joke writer. Unique premises and perspective.

### Bo Johnson -- Two Sailboats
I think Bo is officially based out of Seattle, but he seems to be on the road a lot so I'm not quite sure. I first heard of him when he was in town at Comedy on State opening for Joe Dombrowski, and Peggy Hurley hosted for them, and she raved about Bo. This special shows why. Great unique delivery, fun material and an easy watch.
