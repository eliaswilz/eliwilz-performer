### Caribou
This is what you're thinking of when you're thinking of a cheeseburger. No frills. Perfect size for a bar burger, not too big, served in a paper tray. Greasy and served late. No notes

### Blue Moon
I ate this burger all through the covid pandemic. I lived down the block and had to walk past a liquor store to get there so pick-up orders made for a great friday night tradition. Topped with classic ingredients, too big for the first bite to fit in your mouth, good with mayo. Get the waffle fries for a full meal.

### Train with Mike Burger at Baldwin St. Grille
If most of my favorite burgers are no frills, this one is the exception that breaks the rule. Created in support of local personal trainer Mike Onsrud, this burger is stacked high bacon, pepperjack jalapeño and deep-fried banana peppers. its not too spicy, very sweeet. 

### Everly
Just a great burger, great mouth feel on the bite, just enough resistence when you chew. Great flavor, and one of the only burgers I'd recommend ordering the salad with instead of the fries.
