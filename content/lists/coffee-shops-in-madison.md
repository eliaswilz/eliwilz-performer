### Cargo Coffee
They've got 2 locations (maybe more I'm not going to google it). They've got 2 locations that I go to, the one on S Park St is near my house and has a great and convenient drive-thru. They have a sign that implores you to try a White Mocha and it worked on me. I got it once on a wintery day and went ice skating after and it tasted great. The one one East Wash has a bunch of big tables that I like to go work at, but I usually just order boring coffee there. It's good.

Order: White Mocha

### Wonderstate
Yeah, idk. I used to walk past here every morning and stop in on Thursdays before my radio show and they began remembering my name and order, and I theres and it felt like a great little community. I haven't been since I moved. I used to run into my friend Kevin here.

Order: Cold Brew

### Ancora
This place makes me mad sometimes because its a coffee shop pretending to be a breakfast restaurant so occasionally you'll go in and they'll be like "sure yeah you can order a breakfast sandwich but it won't be here by Christmas" and it's fine, I'll just get a drink. They can do a rasberry vanilla latte that doesn't taste fake.

Order: Raspberry Vanilla Latte
