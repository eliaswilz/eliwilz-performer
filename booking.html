Bio

01

Short Bio

Eli Wilz is a stand-up comedian from Madison, WI. He has opened for comics you have heard of in rooms you think are interesting. He has also opened for comics you haven't heard of in rooms that suck. In both scenarios, everyone thinks he did a really good job.

02

Long Bio

Eli Wilz is a stand-up comedian, improviser, and professional member of Atlas Improv Company in Madison, WI. He's opened for Adam Ray and Johnny Pemberton and been named Best of the Fest at Madison Comedy Week and Headlined the Floodwater Comedy Festival. A seasoned performer, he's appeared at top festivals across North America, including Toronto Sketch Fest, Last Best Comedy Festival, and Milwaukee Comedy Festival. Catch him every Thursday at Cardinal Bar as the halftime act for the Tony Castaneda Latin Jazz Band.
